Travel Tips For Your Trip In British Columbia

Climate & Weather

The province's climate is influenced by the Pacific Ocean and diverse topography from region to region. Generally temperatures are milder on the coasts, warmer in the southern.

Drive in BC

Right-hand side of the road driving. The province allows visitors to drive for up to six months with a valid driver lincence from another province, state or country.

Georgraphy

The province's geography is diversity. The landscapes include mountains, lakes, creeks, rocky coastlines, beaches, forests, inlanddeserts and grassy plains

Public Holiday

Government offices, bank and some stores are closed on Statutory holidays. Check statutory holiday list before scheduling your vacation.

Culture & History

The provice has a unique mix culcutre of european,Asian, and first nations. Two spanish exploer made the first landing to the provice in 1774 & 1775.

Visitor Information & Service

The province's visitor service network covers more than 100 community-owned visitor service centres and booths.
